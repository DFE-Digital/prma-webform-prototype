Before you start

You will need:

  • the number and details of attendance penalty notices sent, including:
    • how many were withdrawn
    • how many lead to prosecution
    • how many were unresolved
  • the number and details of cases in case management, including:
    • how many were withdrawn
    • how many lead to prosecution
  • the number and details of parenting orders granted, including:
    • how many were implemented
    • how many were not implemented because of a lack of provision
    • how many were not implemented because of a breach by a parent
  • the number and details of parenting contracts offered, including:
    • how many were accepted by parents
  • the number of education supervision orders, including:
    • how many were issued in place of prosecution
    • how many were issued as well as prosecution
